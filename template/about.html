About Us

about us

Our company

WP+ID is a Dallas-based design firm specializing in residential and boutique commercial interiors with a unique emphasis on architecture. Formed in 2021, WP+ID works to deliver bespoke solutions that stand the test of time. Value lies in our ability to cultivate connection, ultimately fueling creativity and decisiveness. Our services range from e-design to full scale remodels and new construction, and offer a tailored aesthetic, with an edge.

Led by founder Whitney Perry, Whitney boasts RID and NCIDQ accreditations and holds a BA of Architecture from Texas Tech University. Over the past 14 years, she has worked with award-winning firms across sectors including Corporate, Retail, Hospitality, and Residential design. With her interdisciplinary background, Whitney is set apart by her ability to approach each project holistically, considering architectural influences like balance, scale, and light as well as construction factors such as budget, schedule, and material limitations. An innate ability to connect with people and an eye for the interesting, Whitney creates spaces that are as unique as those who inhabit them. At home, she has three children, two dogs, and two fluffy cats. She knows firsthand the power of intentional design, and with WP+ID she aims to reveal the same for others.

Design Foundations

These foundations guide our design approach.

Details

Details distinguish the extraordinary from the ordinary. From careful material selection to scale and composition, the details we choose contribute to the overarching balance.

Intentionality

We design each space around the pillars of purpose and intentionality to produce interiors that enhance functionality. Our designs are carefully tailored to each client’s specific needs and lifestyle resulting in a space that can be lived in and admired.

Expanse

We embrace the creative challenge of working across the expanse of architecture and construction constraints to create a cohesive design that celebrates all facets of the built environment.

Character

To fuel connection, we enjoy inserting unique and interesting details to infuse character, interest, and personality. We value the uniqueness each client brings and see every project as an opportunity to create something special that captures that unique character.

WP+ID Values

These values embody our ethos.

Connection

We strive to build lasting client relationships rooted in connection. Ultimately, getting to know the people behind the project is just as rewarding as the project itself.
Trust

Our relationships are rooted in trust. We aim to be transparent in approach, leaving room for open communication. With trust established, we open the door for creativity and efficiency.
Authenticity

Authenticity is integral to our relationships. We strive for our clients and associates to sense our genuine interest in their lives and wellbeing. Being authentic also means approaching highs and lows with complete transparency, celebrating successes, but not omitting difficult conversations.
Understanding

Knowing our clients and associates means seeking to understanding their vantage. Kindness is our heart posture, and we seek to relate. In all things, we want to actively listen, so that we may better understand.
